Urostone research center hospital.A name whose credibility is not only in Bihar but also outside Bihar. Over the years, this hospital has instilled its good faith in the hearts of the people. And the reason for this belief is the best treatment and best facilities.

This hospital has made a different identity under the supervision of Dr. Manoj Kumar, the director of the hospital and well-known urologist of the city. Kept alive and today all those people are leading their good life.

Eurostone Research Center Hospital has been very popular among the poor as there is no better treatment and accommodation at affordable rates and nowhere else in the whole of Darbhanga. So that the poor could get help, Dr. Manoj Kumar connected his hospital with the scheme of Ayushman Bharat. All the treatment related to kidney is arranged in Euro Stone Research Center Hospital, dialysis is also done here.
